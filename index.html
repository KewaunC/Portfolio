About Me

Growing up, I've always been told that I was a sponge, since I love to soak up knowledge and experiences, and I also had a knack for allowing those around me to be comfortable. My journey began in the computer lab and library, where I nurtured a profound love for technology, knowledge and effective communication.

Over the past decade, I've immersed myself in the service industry, refining my leadership abilities, fostering teamwork and coaching, along with developing efficient procedures. Notably, my time in Toronto, CA, where I played a pivotal role in the successful launch of a new store, sharpened these skills significantly.

Recently, I graduated from Per Scholas, where I furthered my technical prowess and deepened my understanding of tech policies. I also achieved my CompTIA CySA+ certification, fortifying my capabilities in cybersecurity. Additionally, I am immensely grateful for my time with Resilient Coders, where I learned the true meaning of resiliency and gained valuable software engineering skills. This experience has been instrumental in shaping my approach to technology and problem-solving.

Looking forward, I am eager to expand my knowledge and expertise in the field of cybersecurity. My ultimate goal is to not only safeguard my loved ones but also to share my expertise with others, teaching and mentoring those who are keen to learn.

I believe my diverse background, coupled with my unwavering commitment to personal growth and contribution, positions me as a valuable asset to your team. I'm eager to explore how my unique blend of experiences and aspirations align with your organization's goals and how I can contribute to its success.
